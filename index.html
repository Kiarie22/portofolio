Biography

Early Life

Since childhood I have had a strong desire in technology. Born in Murang’a I was always fascinated by how things worked, often taking apart old radios and computers just curious to know what’s inside.

Education

I attended Stella Maris where I discovered my passion for maths and science. Currently I am pursuing my degree in Agricultural Economics and Rural Development focusing on application of information technology in agribusiness.

My IT Journey

I chose IT because I believe technology is the bridge to the future. My dream is to become a manager and build applications that solve real world problems.
